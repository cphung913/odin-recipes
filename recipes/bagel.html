Bagel

Description

A cream cheese toasted bagel is a delicious and satisfying breakfast or snack option that is easy to prepare. Start by selecting your favorite type of bagel and toasting it until it's golden brown. Next, spread a generous amount of cream cheese over the top of the bagel. You can use plain cream cheese or try a flavored variety, such as onion and chive or strawberry. For an extra burst of flavor, you can also add toppings like smoked salmon, sliced tomato, or avocado. This recipe is versatile and can be customized to your liking, making it a go-to choice for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole bagel
  • 4 tbsp cream cheese

Steps

  1. Take a knife and cut through the center of your bagel
  2. Place both slices of bagel into the toaster
  3. After toasting the bagel, add a generous helping of cream cheese to both sides
  4. Optional: experiment with different toppings, such as jam, peanut butter, salmon, avocado, and more!
