Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A PB and J sandwich is a classic American snack that is quick and easy to make. To start, gather your ingredients: bread, peanut butter, and jelly. Choose your favorite type of bread and spread a generous amount of peanut butter onto one slice. On the other slice, spread a generous amount of your favorite jelly or jam. Press the two slices together and cut the sandwich in half. You can serve it as is, or toast the bread for a warm and toasty treat. This simple recipe is perfect for a quick lunch or an on-the-go snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white or whole wheat bread
  • 1 tbsp smooth or crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp fruit jam or jelly

Steps

  1. Lay out the two slices of bread side by side
  2. Using a butter knife, spread peanut butter on 1 slice of bread
  3. Spread jam or jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. stack the slices on top of one another so that the peanut butter and jelly combine
  5. optional: toast your sandwich in a toaster oven for 90 seconds for an extra crunch!
